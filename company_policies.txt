Company Name: Rooman Tech Solutions

Leave Policy:
- Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid leave per year.
- Casual leave must be applied at least 1 day in advance.
- Sick leave can be availed without prior approval but requires medical proof for more than 2 days.

Work From Home:
- Work from home is allowed 2 days per week with manager approval.
- Employees must be available on Microsoft Teams during working hours.

Probation & Confirmation:
- New employees are on probation for 6 months.
- Performance is reviewed monthly during probation.
- After successful completion, confirmation letter will be issued.

Office Timings:
- Regular working hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Saturday and Sunday are weekly holidays except for critical support teams.

Benefits:
- Health insurance coverage starts after 3 months of joining.
- Employees are eligible for annual bonus after 1 year.

HR Contact:
- For any queries, email hr@roomantech.com
